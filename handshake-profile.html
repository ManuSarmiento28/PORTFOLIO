Handshake Profile

A redesigned student profile that helps recruiters understand what students can actually do.

01

Problem

Students are more than a list of courses, but Handshake profiles often feel surface-level. This project adds proof of skills, tools, projects, and endorsements directly into the profile experience.

User Discovery

Interviews showed that students use Handshake mostly when applying, not as a profile they build over time.

Solution

Each course expands to show skills learned, tools used, related projects, and professor or peer endorsements.

Iterations

The project went through paper prototype, low-fidelity prototype, and final high-fidelity prototype.
